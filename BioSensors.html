Bio Sensors

Based on the work of Nicolae et al."Biohybrid Devices" I replicate the "grow arround method" and "layering" to embed sensing capabilities in bacterial cellullose. In the course of this project I built a total of 3 different sensors, two of which were resistive (temperature and stretch sensors) and one capacitive (mutual capacitance matrix). Using the Arduino and other modules, I was able to design functional bio-hybrid sensors.
