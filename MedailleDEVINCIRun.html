Devinci Run Medal

I modelled this medal during the Devinci Run on SolidWorks. The Devinci Run is an event organised by my school's sports department to promote the Paris 2024 Olympics. The aim was to boost the school's energy through a sporting event that encouraged students to run a certain distance (a choice of 5 or 10 km) to mark this Olympic event. The medal was then given to all the participants in the race to reward them.
